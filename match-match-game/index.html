Match-Match Game

Do you have a good short-term memory? If not - don't be disappointed because you can train it!

How to Play

  1. Press "Choose cards" button.
  2. Enter your name and email.
  3. Choose the difficulty of the game and the type of card shirt you like.
  4. Click "New Game" button and the game starts immediately with the timer.
  5. Select two cards and try to match the picture.
  6. If two pictures matches you can go again.
  7. If two pictures don't match they will turn back.
  8. The player who find all pairs win!

The minimum width of the game field is 550px so you would better to use landscape orientation of your device
